MUSEUM OF CANDY

Dulce de leche candy is a traditional Central and South American favorite. This rich, creamy caramelized milk candy has only one ingredient and is so easy to make! Dulce de leche does have a long cooking time, however, so make sure you leave yourself enough time to make it! This rich candy is good enough to eat on its own, but you'll love it in dulce de leche pecan ​bites, ​dulce de leche truffles,​ ​dulce de leche chocolate cups, or​ ​dulce de leche fudge.
